Solution
The solution to the problem is obvious. The prisoners are seated in a circular fashion, and each of them get a treat in a linear turn after turn basis. This immediately tells us that the problem involves the use of modulo. To solve this we observe that starting from seat s, the distribution is circuar among the prisoners. So, the formula would be; (s + m - 1) % n. There is a corner case to this solution. It happens when the result of the modulo becomes 0. In that case, the answer is n.
